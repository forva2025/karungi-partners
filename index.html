About Karungi & Partners

For over 15 years, Karungi & Partners Advocates & Solicitors has been at the forefront of legal excellence in Uganda, providing comprehensive legal services to individuals, corporations, and institutions across East Africa.

Founded on the principles of integrity, excellence, and client-centered service, our firm has built a reputation for delivering exceptional legal solutions while maintaining the highest standards of professional conduct.
